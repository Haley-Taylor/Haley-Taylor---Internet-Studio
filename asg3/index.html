Framing it in a story format helps refine the idea by forcing you to clearly define the characters and their experience. The approach makes it simpler and allows the designer to dissect various elements, it turns an idea into a thoughtful narrative. It also allows you to get insight into the user's journey so you can identify positive and negative aspects. This format helps designers and guides them to make successful design decisions.

Creative Brief

Problem

College students struggle to maintain their physical and mental health due to busy schedules, limited guidance, and resources.

Audience

College students age 18-25

Competition

The competitors are mental health platforms and wellness apps such as BetterHelp and Headspace.

Key Messages

Our website helps you find balance so you can thrive at campus and in life.

Call to Action

Begin your Wellness Journey!

How to Measure Success

I will measure my success with engagement and behavior. I will see how many people have clicked on resources and analyze how the users behavior changes with the provided resources.
