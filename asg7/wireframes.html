Reading Questions:

What are the advantages and disadvantages of sketching wireframes over wireframe software?

Sketching wireframes offers speed and simplicity. It allows designers to quickly translate ideas onto the page without having to mess with any software. It would promote creativity during early phases. Although it does have some drawbacks. To start it is more difficult to change, you cannot go back and make edits as easily. It can also be messy and harder to share. Wireframe software can be easier to control. It offers cleaner layouts and it can be shared easily. You can also utilize various software features such as layers or interactive elements. The biggest disadvantage of software is it can be time consuming or difficult to navigate.
