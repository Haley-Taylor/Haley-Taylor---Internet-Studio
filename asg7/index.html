Queen Athena

The warrior cat named Athena would make a remarkable ruler. She embodies grace and wisdom like the goddess she is named after. Like the goddess Athena, Athena carries herself with strength and is guided by her intelligence.
She will make a good ruler because she is thoughtful and will lead with fairness. Her feline nature teaches her to be curious and careful. Under her rule the kingdom will erupt with harmony and peace.

Running Mate

Kitty Hermes

Athena's running mate would be kitty Hermes, named after the messenger god. Hermes' communication skills perfectly complement Athena's. While Athena embodies justice, Hermes embodies agility and adaptability.

Her top platform

Athena's platform as queen would center around balance and empathy. She would promote a kingdom where wisdom guides every decision. Athena believes that every voice deserves to be heard. She believes that strength comes from understanding rather than dominance. Her reign would focus on creating peace for all creatures.
